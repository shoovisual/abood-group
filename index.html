About Abood Group

BRAND YOU CAN TRUST

Abood Group of Companies is a family owned business established in 1986 in Morogoro, Tanzania. The group started as a regional trading house dealing in general goods trading in soft commodities.

group then expanded into its flag ship company in Passenger Transportation. As the business continued to grow year on year it diversified into other industries including manufacturing, cargo logistics, Petroleum, real estate, construction and tourism.

Abood Group transformation into a rapidly growing business group is widely considered to be an inspirational success story from its humble beginnings. Today, after 34 years it is a trusted brand the serves thousands of customers daily making it one of the largest business group in terms of customer base in Tanzania.

Mission

Our Mission is to be one of the most respectable and successful corporate groups in East Africa.

OUR COMPANIES

Abood Bus Service Limited

Abood Bus Service Limited is the flagship company of Abood Group and is the largest intercirty bus operator in Tanzania. Founded in 1986, the company has built a solid reputation for quality and reliability, operating a fleet of more than 100 buses that serve the entire country, from the Northern to the Southern, Western and Eastern regions.

MA Cargo Truckers & Forwarders Co. Limited

M.A Cargo Truckers & Forwarders Company Limited was established in 2004. It is a trucking company that has been operating in Tanzania for over 20 years. The company provides end to end logistics solutions for its clients and operates a fleet of over 200 trucks. The company transports petroleum and general cargo from Dar port to Eastern and Southern Africa countries.

Apel Petroleum Limited

Apel Petroleum Limited is an oil marketing company registered in Tanzania in 2006. Its operations are based at Kurasini, Dares salaam. The company handles importation, warehousing, distribution and marketing of petroleum products across Tanzania. The company has expanded its footprint across Tanzania establishing retail stations across the country and intends to become a key player in the petroleum sector in Tanzania.

Bushman Safari Trackers Limited

Bushman Safari Trackers Limited is a safari company operating safari concessions in Tanzania. The company started its operations in 2007 and operates 2 hunting areas including Maswa GR North and Burko Open Area. The company provides some of the best safari camps in Tanzania for clients to visit and has a large market base in North America, Central America and Europe.
